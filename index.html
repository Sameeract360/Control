Manhua

The word manhua was originally an 18th-century term used in Chinese literati painting. It became popular in Japan as manga in the late 19th century.

Manhwa

The author or artist of a manhwa is called a manhwaga (만화가; 漫畵家). They take on the task of creating a comic that fits a certain format.
